My top 5 Semester goals

  1. Goal 1: Improve Academic Performance Why: Enhance knowledge retention and boost GPA. How: Attend all lectures, dedicate 2 hours/day to studying, and seek help from professors when needed.
  2. Goal 2: Enhance Time Management Skills Why: Balance academics, personal life, and extracurricular activities effectively. How: Use a planner, set realistic deadlines, and allocate specific times for each activity.
  3. Goal 3: Develop a New Skill (Coding) Why: Increase career prospects and personal growth. How: Complete online coding courses (e.g., Python, JavaScript) and participate in coding challenges.
  4. Goal 4: Improve Physical and Mental Well-being Why: Boost energy levels and maintain a healthy lifestyle. How: Exercise for 30 minutes, 3 times a week, and practice mindfulness through meditation apps.
  5. Goal 5: Increase Community Engagement Why: Expand professional and social networks. How: Attend at least 2 campus events/month and volunteer for a local community project.
