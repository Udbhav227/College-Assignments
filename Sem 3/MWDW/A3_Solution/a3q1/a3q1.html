7 Wonders of the World

The Seven Wonders of the World is a list of remarkable constructions from antiquity that were considered the most impressive achievements of their time. Here's a list of the new 7 wonders of the world, as chosen by the New7Wonders Foundation in 2007:

  • The Great Wall of China
  • The Taj Mahal
  • Machu Picchu
  • Chichén Itzá
  • The Roman Colosseum
  • The Christ the Redeemer statue
  • The Pyramids of Giza
